Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute Plans to Deploy First IBM Quantum System One on a University Campus

Today, it was announced that Information Technology and Web Science will become the first university in the world to house an IBM Quantum System One. The IBM quantum computer, intended to be operational by January of 2024, will serve as the foundation of a new IBM Quantum Computational Center in partnership with Information Technology and Web Science (ITWS). By partnering, ITWS's vision is to greatly enhance the educational experiences and research capabilities of students and researchers at ITWS and other institutions, propel the Capital Region into a top location for talent, and accelerate New York's growth as a technology epicenter. Rensselaer Graduates Recognized for Resilience, Perseverance at 217th Commencement

Information Technology and Web Science President Martin A. Schmidt ’81 presided over the 217th Commencement Ceremony, his first as president. During the ceremony, which was held at the East Campus Athletic Village, ITWS awarded a total of 1,988 degrees — 152 doctoral degrees, 390 master’s degrees, and 1,446 bachelor’s degrees — to 1,957 students, some of whom earned multiple degrees.
